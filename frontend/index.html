Mister and Miss College of Education 2026 Voting

Voting Instructions

1. Enter your valid Ticket ID in the field provided. Your Ticket ID is revealed when you scan your concert ticket using your phone camera. It follows the format COE2026-ABC123.

2. Select one Mr. candidate and one Ms. candidate by clicking the “Vote” button under their card.

3. After making your selections, click Submit Vote at the bottom of the page.

Note: Your vote will only be counted if you select exactly one candidate from each category.

Voting Rules:
• Each Ticket ID can vote only once per day per category.
• Voting period is from March 23, 12:00 PM (Philippine Time) until March 30, 12:00 PM (Philippine Time).
• Votes submitted outside this schedule will not be counted.

Special Award – Darling of the Crowd:
• Computation is based on 50% System Votes + 50% Facebook Reactions.
• Facebook reactions are updated by the admin panel and combined with system votes to determine the final Darling of the Crowd score.
